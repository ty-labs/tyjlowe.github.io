Most recently, I have accepted a position as a Data Management & Governance Intern at First National Bank Corporation where I am currently working.

Through these experiences, I have become very interested in the software engineering field, and more specifically, in the subset of software engineering that is the back-end, server-side engineering of modern applications. To further pursue these interests I have tacked on a Software Engineering minor at CMU and am also seeking future roles as a software engineer.

Additionally, I have worked on developing softer skills such as business fundamentals (Accounting, Finance, Econ) and communications through my business minor.

Outside of school and work, some activities I enjoy engaging in are skiing/snowboarding, weight lifting, surfing, and playing video games when I get the chance.
